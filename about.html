ABOUT UU DIGITAL

WHY WE DO, WHAT WE DO.

THE PURPOSE

UU Digital is a very small film company that creates cinematic entertainment that is free from inappropriate language, and unhelpful references. We try to make our content as kid-friendly as possible while still telling a complex story that is entertaining to all. On a very low to non-existent budget and with the help of open-source software such as Blender, we can add all the effects that you see in our videos. We release content as often as possible and are always welcoming new ideas (so feel free to submit your scripts and ideas to us!)

THE NAME

We got the name UU Digital from the owner's last name: Whitney. The W in it is what inspired us to use two U’s instead of one W. It is meant to be pronounced as W Digital, yet, since we specialize in visuals and graphics, we decided that two U’s looked a lot better than a single W.

WHY SUPPORT US?

You might be thinking to yourself, why support this small company? I’ll tell you why: We need your help! We need your support to continue what we do! We would love to increase the quality of our work but put quite frankly camera gear is expensive and though the gear is not what makes a great film it sure does help! So consider helping us out, whether it's just by telling your friends about our channel or by supporting us on Patreon, or even something else - we'll take it!

WHAT YOU’LL GET

If you decide to support us you have some perks that might prove to give you some bonus entertainment. By becoming a Patron you get access to deleted scence, bloopers, behind the scenes and even some asset files for those who are themselves interested in filmmaking. If you love what we do want to see this small company boom this is the best thing you can do for now to help us.
